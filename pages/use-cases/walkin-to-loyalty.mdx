# Turning Walk-ins into Loyal Customers

Many retail stores struggle to convert first-time visitors into repeat buyers. Zithara solves this by automating loyalty programs and personalized offers.

## Zithara Solutions
- **Real-Time Visitor Tracking** – Identify customers as they enter your store.
- **Automated Loyalty Enrollment** – Instantly add new visitors to your loyalty program.
- **Personalized GEO-Targeted Offers** – Send SMS/WhatsApp promotions tailored to local store visitors.

## Example
A **Mumbai coffee shop** increased repeat visits by 35% by sending first-time visitors personalized offers based on their neighborhood.
