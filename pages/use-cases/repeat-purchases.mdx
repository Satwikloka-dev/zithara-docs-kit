# Boost Repeat Purchases

Customers often make a single purchase and disappear. Zithara drives repeat sales with automated, personalized campaigns.

## Zithara Solutions
- **Behavior-Based Triggers** – Send campaigns based on purchase history.
- **Personalized Discounts & Offers** – Target customers with relevant promotions.
- **GEO-Targeted Campaigns** – Localized promotions for specific store areas.

## Example
A **Delhi retail chain** increased repeat purchases by sending automated reminders and offers to customers in different city neighborhoods.
