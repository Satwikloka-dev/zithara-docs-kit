# Monitor and Optimize Store Performance

Retailers struggle to track performance across multiple stores. Zithara provides location-wise analytics to help make data-driven decisions.

## Zithara Solutions
- **KPI Dashboards** – Monitor sales, footfall, and campaign performance.
- **GEO Analytics** – Compare downtown vs suburban store performance.
- **Inventory & Staffing Insights** – Optimize resources based on store data.

## Example
A **Chennai retail chain** analyzed store-level KPIs and discovered suburban stores needed more inventory, increasing sales by 20%.
