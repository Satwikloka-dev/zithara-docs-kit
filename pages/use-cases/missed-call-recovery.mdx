# Recover Missed Leads Automatically

Retailers often lose potential customers when calls go unanswered. Zithara automates missed call recovery to ensure no lead slips through.

## Zithara Solutions
- **Automatic Lead Capture** – Capture missed calls instantly.
- **Follow-Up Messages** – Send SMS or WhatsApp reminders automatically.
- **Smart Routing** – Assign leads to the nearest store or agent.

## GEO-Focused Example
A **Bengaluru electronics store** recovered 30% of missed calls by sending automated reminders to customers from nearby localities.
