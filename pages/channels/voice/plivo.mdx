# Plivo Integration (In-House Voice Engine)

Zithara uses Plivo as a **custom in-house voice provider**, not a plug-and-play SDK.

This enables deep control, automation, and business logic.

---

## Why Plivo for Voice Automation

Plivo provides:
- Global calling infrastructure
- Reliable webhooks
- Call recording access
- SIP and PSTN support

Zithara builds intelligence on top of it.

---

## What Zithara Adds on Top of Plivo

- Custom webhook orchestration
- Business-rule driven call flows
- Real-time event processing
- Recording lifecycle management
- CRM-native call context

This is not just integration — it’s a voice framework.

---

## Retail Use Cases

- Automated order confirmations
- Missed-call recovery
- Call-based lead qualification
- Agent routing by priority
- AI-assisted post-call actions

---

## Voice as a Platform Capability

Plivo enables delivery.  
Zithara enables **outcomes**.

Voice becomes measurable, actionable, and scalable.
