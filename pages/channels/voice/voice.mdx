# Voice Channel

Voice is the most powerful engagement channel in retail.

It carries urgency, trust, and intent — things text cannot.

Zithara treats voice as a **core system**, not a legacy add-on.

---

## Why Voice Still Wins in Retail

Voice is critical for:
- High-value purchases
- Appointment confirmations
- Abandoned lead recovery
- Support and escalations
- Relationship-driven sales

Retail conversations close faster on calls.

---

## Voice as an Event Stream

In Zithara, every call generates events:
- Call initiated
- Answered / missed
- Duration
- Outcome
- Recording availability

These events power:
- Automation
- Segmentation
- Analytics
- AI decisioning

- AI-ready call data

Voice is not isolated — it is operational.
