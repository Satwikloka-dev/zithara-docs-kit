# Social Media Channels

Social media is where brand perception is built in public.

Zithara integrates social engagement into the CRM workflow.

---

## Why Social Matters in Retail

Customers:
- Discover brands
- Ask questions
- Leave feedback
- Judge responsiveness

Ignoring social means losing trust.

---

## Social as a CRM Input

Zithara treats social interactions as:
- Customer events
- Engagement signals
- Feedback sources

Not just comments.

---

## Supported Platforms

- Facebook
- Instagram (via Meta)

Social data feeds:
- Segmentation
- Insights
- Automation

---

## From Reaction to Action

Social engagement becomes:
- Follow-up tasks
- CRM conversations
- Reputation workflows

Social is no longer isolated.
