# SMS Channel

SMS is about speed and certainty.

Zithara uses SMS for **high-intent, time-sensitive communication**.

---

## Why SMS Still Matters

SMS is best for:
- OTPs and confirmations
- Alerts and reminders
- Missed-call notifications
- Short transactional updates

When delivery matters, SMS wins.

---

## SMS in Zithara

Zithara supports:
- Event-triggered SMS
- Automation workflows
- CRM-linked messages
- Delivery tracking

SMS is tightly controlled and purposeful.
