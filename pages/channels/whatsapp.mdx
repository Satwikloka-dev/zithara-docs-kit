# WhatsApp Engagement

WhatsApp is the most effective customer engagement channel in modern retail.

Zithara uses WhatsApp as a **conversational automation layer**, not just messaging.

---

## Why WhatsApp Works for Retail

- Extremely high open rates
- Real-time conversations
- Familiar to customers
- Supports rich interactions

WhatsApp is where customers respond.

---

## WhatsApp in Zithara

Zithara enables:
- Campaign broadcasts
- 1:1 conversations
- Automated replies
- Template-based messaging
- Event-triggered outreach

All fully integrated with CRM data.

---

## Automation-First Approach

WhatsApp messages can be triggered by:
- Customer actions
- Segment changes
- Task completions
- Missed calls
- Review submissions

---

## Retail Use Cases

- Post-visit follow-ups
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Appointment reminders
- Feedback collection
- Loyalty communication

WhatsApp becomes a revenue channel, not support-only.
