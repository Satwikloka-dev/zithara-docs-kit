# Email Channel

Email remains critical for long-form, structured, and lifecycle communication.

Zithara treats email as a **strategic engagement channel**, not bulk outreach.

---

## When Email Works Best

Email is ideal for:
- Product education
- Offers and campaigns
- Reports and summaries
- Lifecycle journeys
- Relationship nurturing

---

## Email in Zithara

Zithara enables:
- Automated email campaigns
- Behavior-triggered emails
- Segment-based targeting
- CRM-personalized content
- Performance tracking

---

## Event-Driven Emails

Emails are triggered by:
- Segment transitions
- Inactivity windows
- Purchase events
- Support outcomes

No manual scheduling required.

---

## Email with Intelligence

Email content can be:
- AI-generated
- Context-aware
- Customer-specific

Email becomes relevant again.
