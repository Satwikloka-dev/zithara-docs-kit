# AI-Native Platform

Zithara is built with AI as **infrastructure**, not an afterthought.

AI enhances every layer of the platform.

---

## Beyond Chatbots

AI in Zithara is used to:
- Analyze customer behavior
- Assist with responses
- Generate insights
- Optimize engagement timing

It supports teams rather than replacing them.

---

## Practical AI Use Cases

- Suggested replies for messages
- AI-generated review responses
- Intelligent customer segmentation
- Call summaries and insights

---

## Responsible AI Design

Zithara ensures:
- Human approval where required
- Transparent AI actions
- Brand-safe communication
- Configurable AI usage per location

AI works within clear boundaries.
