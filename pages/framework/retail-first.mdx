# Retail-First by Design

Retail businesses operate differently from B2B or SaaS companies.

Zithara is built specifically for **retail realities**, not adapted from generic CRMs.

---

## The Reality of Retail

Retail teams deal with:
- Walk-in customers
- High-volume, low-context interactions
- Store-level operations
- Distributed sales teams
- Offline-to-online journeys

Most CRMs are not designed for this.

---

## How Zithara Is Different

Zithara models retail as it actually works:
- Customers may appear before they identify themselves
- Conversations start before leads exist
- Stores matter as much as brands
- Speed matters more than pipelines

Every feature is designed with this context in mind.

---

## Store-Level Intelligence

Zithara understands:
- Locations
- Sales staff
- Local campaigns
- Store-specific performance

This allows brands to scale without losing local control.
