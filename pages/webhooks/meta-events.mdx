# Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Integration

Zithara enables retail brands to **manage, engage, and convert conversations from Facebook and Instagram**—directly inside the CRM.

By integrating with **Meta platforms**, Zithara turns social media interactions into **structured customer journeys**, not scattered messages.

---

## Why Meta Matters for Retail

Facebook and Instagram are where customers:
- Discover brands
- Ask questions before visiting a store
- Respond to offers and campaigns
- Engage with ads and posts

Zithara ensures these interactions are **captured, tracked, and acted upon**—not lost in inboxes.

---

## What Zithara Enables with Meta

### Unified Social Inbox
All messages from:
- Facebook Pages
- Instagram Business Accounts

are centralized into Zithara’s CRM, allowing teams to respond faster and with full context.

---

### Lead Capture from Social Conversations

Every conversation can be automatically linked to:
- Customer profiles
- Store locations
- Campaigns or ads

This allows retailers to:
- Identify high-intent prospects
- Track walk-in attribution
- Measure ROI from social engagement

---

### Campaign & Ad-Driven Engagement

Zithara connects social conversations back to:
- Paid campaigns
- Organic posts
- Click-to-message ads

This helps brands understand:
- Which campaigns drive conversations
- Which conversations convert into visits or sales

---

## Automation on Social Channels

Zithara allows businesses to automate key actions based on social interactions:

- Auto-replies for common queries
- Lead assignment to store teams
- Follow-up reminders
- Post-conversation campaigns on WhatsApp or SMS

Automation ensures **speed without losing personalization**.
