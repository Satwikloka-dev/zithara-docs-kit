# Voice Calling & Webhook Events

Zithara Voice Services enable businesses to **connect, automate, and scale customer conversations** using programmable voice calling—fully integrated into the Zithara CRM.

From missed-call handling to AI-assisted outbound calls, Zithara turns voice into a **first-class engagement channel**, not just a utility.

---

## Why Voice Matters in Retail

For retail businesses, voice calls are often:
- The **first point of contact**
- The **last step before conversion**
- The fastest way to resolve high-intent queries

Zithara ensures that **every call is tracked, analyzed, and actionable**, not lost in silos.

---

## What Zithara Voice Provides

### Unified Voice Event Tracking
Zithara captures and standardizes **call lifecycle events** such as:
- Call initiated
- Answered
- Missed
- Hung up
- Failed
- Recording available

All events flow directly into the CRM, enabling automation and insights.

---

### Custom In-House Calling via Plivo

Zithara uses **Plivo** as its **primary in-house calling provider**, allowing us to implement **custom business logic** on top of raw telephony events.

This enables:
- Intelligent call routing
- Context-aware outbound calls
- CRM-linked call tracking
- Fine-grained control over call flows

> Plivo is deeply integrated into Zithara’s voice stack, enabling features that go beyond basic call connectivity.

---

### Multi-Provider Compatibility

Zithara supports multiple calling providers through a **provider-agnostic webhook layer**, allowing businesses to:

- Use existing telephony vendors
- Switch providers without changing workflows
- Run multiple providers in parallel

Supported providers include:
- Exotel
- MyOperator
- Mcube
- Voxbay
- Plivo (in-house)

---

## Webhook-Driven by Design

All voice interactions in Zithara are powered by **real-time webhooks**.

This means:
- Immediate call event visibility
- Reliable call status updates
- Near real-time automation triggers
- No polling or manual reconciliation

Webhook events act as the **source of truth** for all voice activity.

---

## Call Recordings, Made Actionable

Zithara automatically:
- Fetches call recordings from providers
- Stores them securely
- Links them to customers, agents, and conversations

This enables:
- Call audits
- Quality monitoring
- AI-based analysis (sentiment, intent, summaries)
- Compliance and record keeping
