# Leads

Leads represent **intent**, not just data.

In retail, intent often appears before structured information exists.

---

## How Zithara Treats Leads

A lead in Zithara:
- Can originate from calls, messages, or walk-ins
- Can exist without a customer profile
- Can convert automatically or manually

---

## Lead Lifecycle

Leads move through:
- Creation
- Qualification
- Follow-up
- Conversion or closure

Each stage is driven by events, not manual status changes.

---

## Lead-to-Customer Transition

Zithara seamlessly merges:
- Anonymous leads
- Identified customers

ensuring no context is lost.

---

## Retail-Ready Lead Handling

Designed for:
- High lead volume
- Store-level assignment
- Fast response times
- Automation-driven follow-ups
