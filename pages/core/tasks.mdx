# Tasks

Tasks turn intent into action.

They ensure nothing falls through the cracks in high-volume retail environments.

---

## Tasks Are Event-Driven

Tasks are created automatically from:
- Missed calls
- New leads
- Pending follow-ups
- Review alerts

Manual task creation is optional.

---

## Retail-Oriented Task Design

Tasks are:
- Time-bound
- Priority-aware
- Store-assigned
- Agent-specific

---

## Task Automation

Zithara can:
- Auto-assign tasks
- Escalate overdue tasks
- Close tasks based on events

This reduces operational overhead.

---

## Accountability at Scale

Tasks bring structure and accountability without slowing teams down.
