# Customers

A customer in Zithara is **not just a contact**.

It is a continuously evolving profile built from events, conversations, and interactions across channels.

---

## Customers Are Dynamic

Retail customers:
- Appear anonymously
- Engage across channels
- Identify themselves over time

Zithara allows customers to exist **before full identification**.

---

## Customer Identity Resolution

Zithara automatically links:
- Phone numbers
- WhatsApp accounts
- Social profiles
- Store visits

into a single customer profile.

---

## Beyond Static Fields

A customer profile includes:
- Interaction history
- Engagement frequency
- Purchase behavior
- Review activity
- Loyalty indicators

This enables real-time personalization and automation.

---

## Customers as a Foundation

Everything connects to customers:
- Leads originate from them
- Conversations revolve around them
- Events enrich them
- Automation targets them

Customers are the center of the framework.
