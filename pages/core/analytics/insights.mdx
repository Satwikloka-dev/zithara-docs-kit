# Insights Engine

Insights in Zithara are **contextual and actionable**.

They are derived from behavior, not assumptions.

---

## Types of Insights

Zithara generates insights around:
- Customer engagement health
- Lead conversion bottlenecks
- Agent responsiveness
- Store-level performance
- Campaign effectiveness

---

## Real-Time Signals

Insights update as events happen:
- Missed calls
- Delayed follow-ups
- Negative sentiment
- Drop in engagement

This allows immediate intervention.

---

## Insights Power Automation

Insights can:
- Trigger alerts
- Adjust priorities
- Activate workflows
- Guide AI recommendations

---
