# NPS Management

Zithara treats NPS as a **workflow trigger**, not just a metric.

---

## How Zithara Collects NPS

NPS can be collected via:
- WhatsApp
- SMS
- Email
- Post-call follow-ups
- Post-visit surveys

All responses are event-driven.

---

## NPS Categories

Customers are classified as:
- Promoters
- Passives
- Detractors

Classification updates automatically with each response.

---

## Operationalizing NPS

Zithara enables:
- Auto-creation of follow-up tasks
- Escalation for detractors
- Reward flows for promoters
- AI-assisted response suggestions

---

## NPS as a Feedback Loop

NPS feeds into:
- Segmentation
- Automation rules
- Agent performance analysis

It closes the loop between experience and action.
