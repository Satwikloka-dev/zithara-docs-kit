# Users & Teams

Retail is a team sport.

Zithara models users and teams to match real-world operations.

---

## Users in Zithara

Users represent:
- Sales associates
- Store managers
- Support agents
- Admins

Each user has roles and permissions.

---

## Teams and Hierarchies

Teams can be structured by:
- Store
- Region
- Function

This enables precise access control and reporting.

---

## Performance Visibility

Zithara tracks:
- Response times
- Task completion
- Conversation quality

This helps teams improve continuously.

---

## Designed for Collaboration

Zithara ensures teams work together with shared context and clear ownership.
