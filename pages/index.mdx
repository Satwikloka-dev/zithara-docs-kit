# Zithara.ai Documentation

**Zithara.AI** is an AI-powered CRM platform built for retail businesses to **acquire, engage, and retain customers** across online and offline touchpoints.

This documentation is the single source of truth for:
- Product capabilities
- Integrations
- APIs & automations
- Internal and partner usage

---

## What is Zithara.AI?

Zithara.AI helps retail brands convert **walk-in footfall into identifiable customers**, automate engagement, and drive repeat purchases using AI and omnichannel communication.

It brings together:
- In-store lead capture
- Campaign automation
- Customer intelligence
- Review & feedback automation
- Loyalty and retention workflows

All under one unified CRM.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Customer Acquisition
Capture customer data directly from stores using the **Salesman App**, website widgets, QR flows, and integrations.

### Omnichannel Engagement
Run targeted campaigns across:
- WhatsApp
- SMS
- Email
- Facebook & Instagram

All campaigns are behavior-driven and automated.

### AI-Powered Insights
Leverage AI to:
- Analyze customer behavior
- Segment audiences dynamically
- Generate review replies and campaign content
- Optimize engagement timing and messaging

### Loyalty & Retention
Create and manage:
- Loyalty programs
- Coupon campaigns
- Event-based automations (birthdays, anniversaries, visits)

### Feedback & Reviews
Collect and manage:
- Google Business Profile reviews
- AI-assisted replies
- Location-level automation controls

## Integrations

Zithara.AI integrates seamlessly with:

- **ERP systems** for billing and customer sync  
- **Telephony providers** for click-to-call and call tracking  
- **Websites** via embedded forms and scripts  
- **WhatsApp Business API** for customer communication  
- **Social platforms** like Facebook and Instagram
