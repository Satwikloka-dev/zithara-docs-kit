# Reputation & Trust Management

In retail, **reputation is revenue**.

Customers decide where to visit, whom to trust, and what to buy *before* they walk into a store.

Zithara treats reputation as an **operational system**, not a passive metric.

---

## Why Reputation Matters in Retail

Retail customers:
- Read reviews before visiting
- Judge brands by public responses
- Share experiences instantly
- Influence others locally

Ignoring reputation means losing demand upstream.

---

## Reputation as a Framework Layer

In Zithara, reputation:
- Is event-driven
- Feeds segmentation
- Triggers automation
- Influences AI decisions

It is tightly integrated with CRM and engagement workflows.

---

## What Zithara Manages

- Public reviews
- Private feedback
- Sentiment signals
- Response workflows
- Trust recovery actions
