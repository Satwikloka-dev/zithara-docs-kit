# Support Bots

Support bots handle repetitive queries while maintaining brand tone and accuracy.

Zithara support bots are designed for **retail-specific support scenarios**.

---

## What Support Bots Handle

- Order status queries
- Store timings and location
- Return and exchange policies
- Appointment confirmations
- Basic troubleshooting

---

## Context-Aware Responses

Support bots:
- Access customer history
- Understand previous interactions
- Reference recent purchases or calls
- Adapt responses based on sentiment

No generic replies.

---

## Human Handoff

When needed, bots:
- Create support tickets
- Assign tasks to agents
- Transfer conversations seamlessly

Automation without customer frustration.

---

## Supported Channels

Support bots work across:
- WhatsApp
- Web chat
- Email
- Social messaging

Consistent support everywhere.
