# Sales Bots

Sales bots focus on **conversion and engagement**, not just conversation.

They act as digital sales assistants.

---

## What Sales Bots Do

- Qualify leads
- Answer product questions
- Share catalogs or offers
- Schedule callbacks or visits
- Nudge inactive leads

---

## Event-Driven Selling

Sales bots activate when:
- A lead goes cold
- A customer revisits
- A campaign is launched
- A high-intent action is detected

They sell at the right moment.

---

## Personalization at Scale

Sales bots personalize using:
- RFM segments
- Past purchases
- Channel preference
- Location context

Every message feels intentional.

---

## Outcome-Focused Automation

Sales bots don’t just talk — they:
- Move leads forward
- Create tasks
- Trigger follow-ups
- Improve conversion velocity
