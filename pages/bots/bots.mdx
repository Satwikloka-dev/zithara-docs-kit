# AI Bots & Agents

Bots in Zithara are **not chat widgets**.

They are **event-driven AI agents** embedded directly into retail workflows.

---

## Why Bots Matter in Retail

Retail businesses deal with:
- High volume, low patience customers
- Repetitive interactions
- Missed follow-ups
- Agent dependency

Bots reduce friction without removing humans.
