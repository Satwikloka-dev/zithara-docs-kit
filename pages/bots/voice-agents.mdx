# Voice Agents

Voice agents combine AI with telephony to handle real conversations at scale.

Zithara voice agents are built for **retail calling workflows**.

---

## Why Voice Agents Matter

Voice is critical for:
- High-value transactions
- Urgent follow-ups
- Missed-call recovery
- Relationship-driven sales

AI voice agents ensure no opportunity is missed.

---

## What Voice Agents Can Do

- Answer inbound calls
- Make outbound follow-ups
- Collect structured responses
- Route calls intelligently
- Trigger post-call workflows

---

## AI + Telephony Integration

Voice agents integrate with:
- Plivo calling infrastructure
- CRM customer data
- Automation rules
- Call analytics and recordings

They understand context before speaking.

---

## Human Collaboration

Voice agents:
- Assist human agents
- Pre-qualify conversations
- Hand off seamlessly
- Reduce agent workload

Voice AI works *with* your team.
